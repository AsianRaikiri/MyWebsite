My personal story:

I am 21 years old, I have just started studying Computer Science at the University of Applied Science in Karlsruhe. After a few years of not really knowing what I wanted to do with my life, I started to try to get an Idea of what my future should look like. After a lot of thinking and changing between interests, I finally noticed how much joy I had at creating something I can call my own. But since I suck at creative writing or arts in general, I settled for programming. Another helpful bonus was, that this way I learn a lot more about how the most important part of my life, the Internet, actually works.
